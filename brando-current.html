how it works

the powershell script automates everything. one command, zero hassle.

  • 1.auto‑detects all connected flash drives
  • 2.select your target drive from the list
  • 3.formats & partitions the drive automatically
  • 4.downloads tools, isos, drivers, and scripts from original or reliable sources
    (checks hashes where available)
  • 5.organizes everything into a clean folder structure
  • 6.makes bootable via ventoy — ready to use as your ultimate tech toolkit
